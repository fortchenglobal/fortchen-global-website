FortChen Global LLC is a North Carolina–based company dedicated to connecting global craftsmanship with modern retail platforms.

Our company develops products inspired by cultural heritage, including the YUANYI Heritage Collection, which transforms traditional embroidery motifs from Yuanyang, Yunnan into contemporary fashion and lifestyle designs.

We aim to introduce meaningful cultural artistry to a global audience while maintaining quality, authenticity, and modern design principles.
