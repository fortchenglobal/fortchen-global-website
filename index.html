About FortChen Global

FortChen Global LLC is a U.S.-based design and retail company developing modern fashion and lifestyle products inspired by traditional embroidery from Yuanyang, Yunnan. Our collections reinterpret cultural heritage into contemporary apparel, accessories, and design pieces for the global market.
